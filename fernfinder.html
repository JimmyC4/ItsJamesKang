PROJECT GOALS

The project goals for Fernfinder were based on research into how novice plant owners currently buy and easily become discouraged by failure. By studying other companies and websites, such as Bunnings, we gained an understanding of common conventions, techniques and necessities for a success website. Below are some of the project goals for Fernfinder:


  • ■   Not only to sell, but to educate users on how to care for different plants
  • ■   Allow users to easily purchase and create an account
  • ■   Create a memorable experience through visuals, interactions and animations
  • ■   Motivate and bring confidence to our users as new plant owners

PERSONAS

Personas provide ficticious but representative insight into the wants, needs and fustrations of the user. This information helps guide me on what problems must be solved, what elements are essential to the user and website, and how I can best solve users wants, needs and fustrations. Understanding who I am designing for allows me to cater the website around the user, instead of focusing soley around the product or service.
