PROJECT GOALS

THE GIST:

The project goals for Fernfinder were founded on research on the user experience of novice plant owners, who had previously bought plants and had become discouraged after thier plant died.

By researching other companies and websites, such as Bunnings, we gained insight into common conventions, techniques and necessities for a success website.

PROJECT GOALS:

• Not only to sell, but to educate users on how to care for different plants
• Allow users to easily purchase and create an account
• Create a memorable experience through visuals, interactions and animations
• Motivate and bring confidence to our users as new plant owners

PERSONAS

UTILISING PERSONAS

Personas provide ficticious but representative insight into the wants, needs and fustrations of the users. Gathering this information helps guide me on what problems must be solved, what elements are essential to the user, client and website, and how I can best solve user wants, needs and fustrations.

Empathising for who I am designing for allows me to design with the user in mind, instead of focusing too much on the product or service.

Here are some of the personas from this project:

BRIAN

Brian is a university student who visits his local Bouldering gym three times a week. He's dedicated to the sport and has bought his own shoes, chalk, and sports clothes to help him climb.

He visits both alone and with friends on occasion. Brian also works a part-time job to pay his bills and for bouldering expenses.

• University Student
• AGE: 23
• GENDER: Male

JESSICA

Jessica works a full-time job at a business firm. She spends most of her days working, but likes to relax at her local bouldering gym from time to time.

She rarely invites her friends to join, as she finds solving walls relaxing by herself and see's it as a fun alternative to going to the gym. She occasional talks to the other gym climbers she meets.

• Office Worker
• AGE: 30
• GENDER: Female

TIMMY

Timmy visits the bouldering gym with his father every Saturday morning, where they learn to climb walls they haven’t tried before, and to learn a few new tricks.

Timmy enjoys the challenge of studying the walls and loves to talk about bouldering with his friends and family.

• Highschool Student
• AGE: 16
• GENDER: Male

MY TOOLS

Figma is the first tool I use when designing a website. Used for ideating, collating research, designing and even prototyping. Photoshop, AfterEffects and Illustartor are used for more complicated website elements.

Lastly for developement, I used HTML, CSS and Javascript alongside React to build the interactive final design.

BOLD AND BRIGHT

COLOUR PALETTE

Bright and bold colours, representing the bright bouldering holds and fun nature of the sport. The bold colours are contrasted against the slightly saturated and desaturated black and whites, as to not harm the eyes over long viewing sessions.

The colours also appeal to our major target audience of kids and young adults.

Our solution was to incorporate both the guide and shopping process of traditional plant selling websites, to create a one stop shop where users can not only buy their plants, but learn to grow that specific plant with guides, plant tips and personalized quizzes.
